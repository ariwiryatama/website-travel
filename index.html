What About Bromo?

Bromo, an active volcano located in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park, East Java, Indonesia, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Indonesia. This mountain is famous for its spectacular sunrise views, where tourists can see the sun rising over the sea of ​​sand and mist that surrounds the crater.

What About Bali?

Bali, an island located in Indonesia, is famous for its nickname "Island of the Gods". Known for its natural beauty, Bali offers exotic beaches, green terraced rice fields and stunning mountains. Apart from that, this island is also rich in culture and tradition, with thousands of sacred temples and lively religious ceremonies.
